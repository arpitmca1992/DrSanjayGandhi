Our Expertise

Specialised Cardiac Procedures

CABG Surgery
Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting — restoring blood flow to the heart using arterial/venous grafts. 5000+ cases performed.
Valve Repair & Replacement
Mitral, aortic, tricuspid valve repair and mechanical/bioprosthetic replacement for rheumatic & degenerative disease.
Congenital Heart Surgery
Correcting birth defects — ASD, VSD, TOF, TGA. World record: 470g baby survived open-heart surgery at 15 days old.
Aortic Surgery
Ascending aorta replacement, Bentall procedure, aortic dissection repair. First redo aortic aneurysm surgery in Rajasthan.
Heart Failure Surgery
Advanced surgical options for end-stage heart failure including ventricular remodeling and mechanical circulatory support.
ECMO Support
Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation — 50+ lives saved. Pioneering ECMO program in Southern Rajasthan for cardiac & respiratory failure.
LVAD Implantation
Left Ventricular Assist Device — mechanical pump supporting weakened hearts as bridge-to-transplant or destination therapy.
Heart Transplant
Complete heart replacement surgery for end-stage cardiac disease — the ultimate life-saving cardiac procedure.
TAVR (Minimally Invasive)
Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement — valve implantation without opening the chest. Successfully performed on 72yr patient in 2020.

About Dr. Sanjay Gandhi
About

Dr. Sanjay Gandhi

A leader in Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgery with over two decades of experience. Trained at AIIMS New Delhi and SCTIMST Trivandrum, Dr. Gandhi is currently the Head of the Department of CTVS at Geetanjali Medical College & Hospital, Udaipur.

He has performed more than 13,000 complex cardiac surgeries with a focus on patient safety, innovation, and long-term outcomes.
