A distinguished Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgeon with over two decades of clinical and surgical experience. Trained at AIIMS New Delhi and SCTIMST Trivandrum, Dr. Gandhi is currently the Head of the Department of CTVS at Geetanjali Medical College & Hospital, Udaipur.

He has performed more than 13,000 complex cardiac surgeries with a focus on patient safety, innovation, and long-term outcomes.
