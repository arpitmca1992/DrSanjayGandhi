About Dr. Sanjay Gandhi
About

Dr. Sanjay Gandhi

A leader in Cardiothoracic & Vascular Surgery with over two decades of experience. Trained at AIIMS New Delhi and SCTIMST Trivandrum, Dr. Gandhi is currently the Head of the Department of CTVS at Geetanjali Medical College & Hospital, Udaipur.

He has performed more than 13,000 complex cardiac surgeries with a focus on patient safety, innovation, and long-term outcomes.
