World Record — World's Tiniest Baby Heart Surgery 15-day-old infant, 470 grams — successfully operated by Dr. Sanjay Gandhi

In practice since 2004, Dr. Sanjay Gandhi is trained at AIIMS New Delhi and Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute (SCTIMST), Trivandrum. He has previously served at Fortis Hospital, Mohali and Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, and is now Head of the Department of CTVS at Geetanjali Medical College & Hospital, Udaipur.

Recognized among the best cardiac surgeons in India, he has received numerous national and international awards. Patient satisfaction and surgical outcomes remain his foremost priority — with more than 13,000 complex cardiac procedures completed to date.

Specialised Cardiothoracic & Vascular Procedures

Heart Transplant
Heart transplantation represents the most effective long-term treatment for advanced heart failure; however, the supply of donor hearts is limited.
LVAD
Intractable ventricular arrhythmias not responding to cardiac defibrillators and aggressive pharmacologic regimens. High-risk reparative cardiac operation.
ECMO
Clinical pathway and protocols for ECMO (PanMax) in cardiac failure. Indication for ECMO in adult cardiac failure is cardiogenic shock.
Adult Cardiac
If one has coronary artery disease, wherein one or more of the main blood vessels feeding the heart is blocked, coronary artery bypass surgery may be required.
